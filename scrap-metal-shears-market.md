<p><p>The scrap metal shears market comprises four main types: hydraulic, electric, pneumatic, and manual shears. Hydraulic shears utilize hydraulic pressure for powerful cutting, ideal for heavy-duty operations. Electric shears offer convenience and speed, suitable for lighter materials. Pneumatic shears leverage compressed air for efficient cutting, often used in industrial settings. Manual shears, operated by hand, provide portability and are suitable for smaller jobs. Each type caters to specific operational needs and budgetary considerations in the scrap metal industry.</p></p>
