<p><p>The specialty contact lenses market includes three main types: rigid, hybrid, and soft contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses, also known as gas permeable lenses, provide clear vision and are durable but may require an adjustment period for users. Hybrid contact lenses combine a rigid center for sharp vision with a soft outer skirt for comfort. Soft contact lenses are the most popular, offering comfort and ease of use, available in various designs to address different vision needs and lifestyles.</p></p>
