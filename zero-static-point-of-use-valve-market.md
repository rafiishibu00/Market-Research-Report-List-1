<p><p>Zero static point of use valves are essential components in fluid control systems, categorized into three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual valves require human operation for flow control, making them simple and cost-effective. Pneumatic valves utilize compressed air for actuation, allowing for faster response and remote operation. Electric valves leverage electric signals for precise control, offering increased automation and integration with smart systems. Each type suits different applications based on factors like speed, precision, and operational requirements.</p></p>
