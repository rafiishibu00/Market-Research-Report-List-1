<p><ul><li>Potting PV Junction Box</li><li>Non-Potting PV Junction Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV junction box market is primarily divided into two types: potting and non-potting junction boxes. Potting PV junction boxes are filled with a protective resin, offering enhanced durability and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for harsh conditions. Non-potting PV junction boxes, on the other hand, allow for easier maintenance and repair, as they are not sealed with resin. Each type caters to specific application needs, balancing factors like cost, protection, and accessibility.</p></p>
