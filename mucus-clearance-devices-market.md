<p><p>Mucus clearance devices enhance respiratory health by facilitating the removal of mucus from the airways. HFCWO (High-Frequency Chest Wall Oscillation) devices use rapid vibrations to loosen mucus. OPEP (Oscillatory Positive Expiratory Pressure) devices leverage oscillatory pressure to help clear secretions. MCA (Mechanical Cough Assist) devices simulate a natural cough to expel mucus effectively. IPV (Intrapulmonary Percussive Ventilation) devices deliver rapid pressure pulses, aiding in mucus clearance. PEP (Positive Expiratory Pressure) devices maintain airway pressure to improve mucus expulsion during exhalation.</p></p>
