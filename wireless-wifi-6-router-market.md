<p><p>The WiFi 6 router market comprises three main types: Single Band, Dual Band, and Tri Band routers. Single Band routers operate on a single frequency, typically 2.4 GHz, suitable for basic tasks like web browsing. Dual Band routers support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz bands, offering better performance and reduced interference for various applications. Tri Band routers expand on this by adding an additional 5 GHz band, which enhances connectivity for multiple devices, making them ideal for high-demand environments.</p></p>
