<p><p>Cardiac monitors come in various types catering to different patient needs. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG monitoring over 24-48 hours, capturing heart rhythm variations during daily activities. Event monitors record heart activity during specific events, often used for intermittent symptoms. Mobile cardiac monitors offer real-time data transmission via smartphones or wireless connections, enhancing remote patient management. Other types may include implantable monitors or telemetry devices, broadening the scope of cardiac arrhythmia monitoring and facilitating timely interventions.</p></p>
