<p><p>The concrete admixtures market is categorized into two main types: mineral admixtures and chemical admixtures. Mineral admixtures include materials like fly ash, silica fume, and slag that improve concrete's strength and durability. Chemical admixtures, on the other hand, consist of substances that enhance the properties of fresh or hardened concrete, such as plasticizers, superplasticizers, and accelerators. Together, these admixtures optimize concrete performance, reduce water content, improve workability, and extend the lifespan of structures.</p></p>
