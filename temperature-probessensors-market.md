<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Temperature Sensors</li><li>Contact Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature probes/sensors market is divided into two primary types: non-contact and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors use infrared technology to measure surface temperatures from a distance, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. In contrast, contact temperature sensors require direct contact with the object to provide accurate readings, commonly utilizing thermocouples or thermistors for applications in industries like manufacturing and healthcare. Both types serve critical roles in monitoring and controlling temperature across various sectors.</p></p>
