<p><strong>The Digital Cine Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prime Lenses</li><li>Zoom Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital cine lenses market comprises two primary types: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses feature a fixed focal length, offering superior optical quality and wide apertures, making them ideal for capturing sharp, high-contrast images in low-light conditions. In contrast, zoom lenses provide versatile focal lengths within a single lens, allowing filmmakers to adjust perspective without changing lenses. Each type serves distinct creative needs in cinematography, balancing between flexibility and image quality in different shooting situations.</p></p>
