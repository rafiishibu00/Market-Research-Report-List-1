<p><ul><li>Cation Resin</li><li>Anion Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uniform resins market is primarily divided into two types: cation resins and anion resins. Cation resins are designed to attract and exchange positively charged ions, making them essential for water softening and purification applications. In contrast, anion resins target negatively charged ions, playing a critical role in deionization and specific contaminant removal. Both types are crucial in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, ensuring efficient ion exchange and effective separation processes.</p></p>
