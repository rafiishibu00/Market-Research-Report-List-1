<p><p>The concrete surface deactivators market is categorized into organic and inorganic agents, each serving to enhance the aesthetic and functional properties of exposed aggregate concrete. Organic agents, derived from natural compounds, effectively delay the curing of cement surfaces, allowing for easier exposure of aggregates without harmful side effects. Inorganic agents, often mineral-based, utilize chemical reactions to achieve similar effects but may offer increased durability and compatibility with various concrete mixes. Both types are essential for achieving desired finishes and maintaining structural integrity.</p></p>
