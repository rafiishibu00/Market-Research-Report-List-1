<p><strong>The Airplane Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Ply</li><li>Radial Ply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airplane tire market is primarily divided into two types: bias ply and radial ply. Bias ply tires feature layers of fabric plies arranged at various angles, providing flexibility and durability, making them suitable for general aviation. In contrast, radial ply tires have plies arranged radially, offering enhanced performance, lower rolling resistance, and improved fuel efficiency, ideal for commercial and high-performance aircraft. Both types cater to different aviation needs, influencing their application in various flight operations.</p></p>
