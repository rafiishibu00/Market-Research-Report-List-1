<p><p>The tire building machinery market includes several key equipment types essential for tire production. Screw extruders are used for the continuous feeding and shaping of rubber compounds, ensuring consistent material flow. Forming machines shape the tire components, such as treads and sidewalls, into their final configurations. Vulcanizing machines facilitate the curing process, where heat and pressure transform the rubber into a durable finished product. Together, these machines enable efficient and precise tire manufacturing across various applications.</p></p>
