<p><p>The thermal fogger market comprises three main types: gas foggers, fuel foggers, and electric foggers. Gas foggers utilize propane or natural gas to generate heat, creating a dense fog ideal for pest control or sanitation. Fuel foggers operate on gasoline or diesel, offering high mobility and extended operational times. Electric foggers use electrical power to heat the solution and produce fog, providing a quieter and more environmentally friendly option for indoor and outdoor applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on efficiency and usage scenarios.</p></p>
