<p><p>Rotational seismometers are specialized instruments used to detect and measure rotational ground motion during seismic events. The market features two primary types: single-axis and three-axis. Single-axis seismometers measure rotation around one horizontal axis, making them simpler and more cost-effective for specific applications. In contrast, three-axis seismometers capture rotation across three orthogonal axes, providing comprehensive data on ground motion in all directions. This versatility makes three-axis systems suitable for detailed seismic monitoring and analysis in diverse geological environments.</p></p>
