<p><p>The motorized valves market includes various types such as ball valves, butterfly valves, and knife gate valves. Ball valves offer quick shut-off and are ideal for on/off control in pipelines. Butterfly valves are compact and suitable for throttling or regulating flow, making them versatile for various applications. Knife gate valves feature a sharp-edged blade that slices through the material, effectively isolating the flow, primarily used in slurry and bulk material handling. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on operational requirements.</p></p>
