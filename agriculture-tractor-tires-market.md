<p><strong>The Agriculture Tractor Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias-ply Tires</li><li>Radial-ply Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture tractor tires market primarily features two main types: bias-ply tires and radial-ply tires. Bias-ply tires consist of layers of fabric plies that crisscross at varying angles, providing excellent durability and puncture resistance, making them suitable for off-road conditions. In contrast, radial-ply tires have plies that run radially, offering improved traction, reduced rolling resistance, and enhanced fuel efficiency. These variations cater to different farming applications, impacting performance, comfort, and handling in diverse agricultural environments.</p></p>
