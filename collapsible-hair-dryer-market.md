<p><p>The collapsible hair dryer market features two primary types: centrifugal and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers utilize a fan to draw air in and expel it at high speed, resulting in efficient drying and styling. Axial hair dryers, on the other hand, push air through an axial fan, offering a more direct airflow ideal for focused drying. Both designs benefit from a collapsible feature, enhancing portability and convenience, making them popular choices for travel and compact storage.</p></p>
