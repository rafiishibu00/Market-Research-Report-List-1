<p><p>The drilling fluid additives market includes various essential components that enhance the performance and efficiency of drilling operations. Fluid viscosifiers improve the thickness and stability of the drilling mud. Corrosion inhibitors protect equipment from rust and deterioration. Dispersants enhance the uniform distribution of solid particles in the fluid. Surface modifiers alter the properties of the fluid to optimize adhesion and fluid loss. Defoamers reduce gas bubble formation, while biocides control microbial growth, ensuring fluid integrity and operational efficiency.</p></p>
