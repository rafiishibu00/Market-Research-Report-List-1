<p><p>The door dampers market can be categorized into two main types: surface applied door dampers and concealed door dampers. Surface applied door dampers are mounted on the surface of the door frame, making them easily accessible for installation and maintenance. In contrast, concealed door dampers are integrated within the door or frame, offering a more aesthetically pleasing and discreet option. Both types serve to control door movement, enhancing safety and functionality while improving energy efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
