<p><p>The High Molecular Weight Polyisobutylene (HMPIB) market is primarily divided into two types: Controlled-Process Polyisobutylene (C-PIB) and High-Retention Polyisobutylene (HR-PIB). C-PIB is characterized by consistent molecular weight and tailored processing conditions, making it ideal for applications requiring specific viscosity and performance standards. HR-PIB, on the other hand, offers enhanced retention properties, making it suitable for high-performance applications in lubricants, adhesives, and sealants. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs by providing unique advantages.</p></p>
