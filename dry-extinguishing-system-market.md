<p><strong>The Dry Extinguishing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Dry chemical suppression systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry extinguishing systems are crucial for fire safety in environments where water-based systems may be ineffective. The dry sprinkler systems utilize compressed air or nitrogen to keep water in the piping system from freezing, making them ideal for unheated spaces. Dry chemical suppression systems, on the other hand, deploy dry chemicals to extinguish fires by interrupting the chemical reaction of combustion. These systems are effective for various fire types and are commonly used in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
