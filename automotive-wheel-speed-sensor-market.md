<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Sensor</li><li>Active Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel speed sensor market comprises two main types: passive and active sensors. Passive sensors operate using electromagnetic induction and generate a voltage signal based on wheel rotation, typically integrating with anti-lock braking systems (ABS). In contrast, active sensors utilize electronic components to emit signals, requiring an external power source, and offer enhanced accuracy and responsiveness, making them suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Both types are essential for maintaining vehicle safety and performance in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
