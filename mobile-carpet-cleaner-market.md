<p><ul><li>Corded</li><li>Cordless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile carpet cleaner market can be divided into two main types: corded and cordless cleaners. Corded mobile carpet cleaners are powered by an electrical outlet, offering consistent suction and cleaning performance for extended periods. They are typically more powerful and suitable for larger spaces. In contrast, cordless mobile carpet cleaners operate on battery power, providing greater mobility and convenience for quick clean-ups without the hassle of cords. However, they may have limited run time and suction compared to their corded counterparts.</p></p>
