<p><strong>The Fly Ash Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F</li><li>Class C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fly ash is categorized into two primary types based on its chemical composition and properties: Class F and Class C. Class F fly ash is produced from burning anthracite or bituminous coal, characterized by low calcium content and high silica, making it suitable for concrete production and soil stabilization. Class C fly ash, derived from lignite or sub-bituminous coal, contains higher calcium levels, promoting self-cementing properties, which are beneficial for use in cement and concrete applications. Both types enhance durability and workability in construction.</p></p>
